Music Covers

As an assignment, I was given the limitations of a 30x15" page and asked to design a poster about the impact Alex Steinweiss' album cover designs had on design history.

I chose to focus on music packaging, from the covers of old music scores to the modern fascination with buying albums, and structured my poster around the iconic square ratio used in album covers.
