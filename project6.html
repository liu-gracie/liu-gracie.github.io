GoRace

As a fun consideration, I designed a logo for a fictional automobile company, GR. The name stems from the ficticious company's tagline: Go Race! (Which also happens to be a play on my name, Grace.)

The lettering of the logo was initially handdrawn, loosely based off of my favorite serif typeface, then scanned into Adobe Illustartor.
