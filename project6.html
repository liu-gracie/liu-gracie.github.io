turn me on

Tasked with the mission of capturing the essence of an object without directly depicting it, I went through several iterations to design a poster of an incandescent lightbulb.

For my final design, I explored a photomontage process, photographing a lightbulb and vinyl stickers on clear acetate sheets, then combining the images together digitally.
