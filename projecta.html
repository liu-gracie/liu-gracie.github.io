Step Ladder Shelf

In my Metal Furniture for Design class, I designed and built a step ladder that also serves as a shelf and storgae space. The top shelf allows for this piece of furniture to be used as a standing desk, and the compartment created with the cloth can be used for storing pillows and clothes.

Built with metal tubing, thrifted fabric, and white pine wood scraps.
