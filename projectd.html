Modular Crate Chairs

These chairs also serve as a double function as storage units, able to fit together and connect into a wider bench or larger shelf when pieced together. The economical design and construction process is friendly for beginners to replicate.

Built with white pine 1x2" planks, thrifted fabric, and webbing.

I also sewed a matching top with the extra thrifted fabric.
