2Designers

This research project covers the art histories of two Japanese designers, Yusaku Kamekura and Asao Tokolo. The timeline, formatted as an accordian book, spans over major historical events and art movements that have influenced these designers.

The checkered layout is inspired by Tokolo's pattern designs and themes of connection; the cover references a poster designed by Kamekura.
