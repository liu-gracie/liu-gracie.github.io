Grace Liu

Grace Liu is a designer and student, currently majoring in graphic design at the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) and taking classes at Brown University. She is interested in a wide variety of design practices—identity and branding, printing and publishing, and front-end web development—drawing inspiration from stories, symbolism, and systems.

Additionally, Grace is the Creative Director of The Brown Political Review, a student-organized magazine published bi-annually. Grace also engages with other artistic practices such as illustration, painting, and three-dimensional works.
