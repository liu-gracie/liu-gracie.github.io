Normal People

I've taken an excerpt from "Normal People" by Sally Rooney and set the type and book layout in an unconventional manner, creating a reading experience that highlights the nuanced tensions and emotions in the dialouge.

This book can be read normally or as a flipbook with the page divided into two sections. The overall book dimensions are 6in x 3in x 0.5in.
