Some Problems I Have With series

These dust jackets and book covers are for a ficticious book series that explores the various types of online "aesthetic" trends. This proof of concept project explores the problematic aspects of each aesthetic but also what can be learned from each trend.

Through the process, I experimented with working in a visual system while utilizing typographic choices to express the variety and visual distinctions of each aesthetic.
